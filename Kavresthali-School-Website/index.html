Principal's Welcome

Our School

A school rooted in heritage and designed for what comes next — eight ideas that hold us together.

Our Campus

A four-acre campus in Kavresthali-7, Kathmandu with twenty-eight classrooms, four science labs, two computer labs and an open amphitheatre for assembly.

Our Mission

To nurture confident, principled young people who lead with knowledge, work with discipline, and contribute with service — Vidya, Anushasan, Seva.

NEB Curriculum

Aligned with Nepal's National Curriculum Framework from Grade 1 through 12, with English-medium instruction beginning at Grade 4.

Forty Years

Founded in 1985 as a single-room community classroom for twenty-eight children — today serving the families of nineteen surrounding villages.

Transport Network

Nineteen GPS-tracked bus routes covering Tokha, Tarakeshwar, Balaju and beyond — with live tracking inside the parent portal.

Future-Ready

Robotics, AI literacy, and a coding club running from Grade 6. Our STEM team brought silver at the 2026 national NAST youth challenge.

Community First

Need-based scholarships covering full fees for 38 students this year, free uniform support, and a daily mid-day meal for primary grades.

Safe & Inclusive

A no-tolerance policy on bullying, trained student counsellors on campus daily, and a transparent grievance system with anonymous reporting.
