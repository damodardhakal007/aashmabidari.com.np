## Description

**Short Description:**  
Access Nepal's essential government services, news, finance, AI tools, and more from one app.

**Full Description:**  
DAURE is your all-in-one gateway to Nepal's digital services. Browse government portals, verify documents, track finances, play games, listen to radio stations, and access AI platforms – all from a single, beautifully designed app.

**Key Features:**
• 🏛️ Government Services - Access official Nepal portals, passport services, PAN verification, and more
• 📄 Document Verification - Check PAN, Voter ID, License, NEA Bill online
• 💰 Finance & Stock Market - TMS, Mero Share, NEPSE tools, and SIP registration
• 🤖 AI Platforms - ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and more
• 📻 Radio Stations - Listen to Radio Nepal, Kantipur, BBC Nepali, and others
• 📰 News - Stay updated with BBC, Reuters, Online Khabar, Ratopati, and more
• 🎮 Games - Play browser-based games including Chess, 2048, Tetris
• 📺 TV Channels - Watch Nepal TV, Kantipur TV, AP1 HD and international channels
• 🌐 Built-in Browser - Browse any website with session timer
• 🌙 Dark/Light Mode - Choose your preferred theme
• 🇳🇵 Nepali/English - Full bilingual support
• 📊 Activity Tracking - Monitor your usage across services
• ⏱️ Floating Timer - Track time spent on each service
• 🌤️ Weather - Real-time weather based on your location
• ⏰ Analogue & Digital Clock - Beautiful clock display

DAURE brings together everything a Nepali digital citizen needs in one sleek, fast app. Whether you're checking your PAN number, trading stocks, reading news, or just browsing the web – DAURE has you covered.
